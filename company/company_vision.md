# Company Vision

Build software with an AI-native operating model that is fast, high quality, repeatable, and commercially disciplined.

## Mission
Use AI agents as specialized workers inside a governed software company, while the founder acts as CEO, principal strategist, and final approver.

## Success criteria
- Ship products faster without sacrificing code quality
- Maintain clear architecture, documentation, and rollback safety
- Reuse the operating system across multiple software products
- Avoid AI slop through strict quality gates
- Build toward products capable of reaching meaningful ARR

## Founder role
The founder:
- sets vision
- approves priorities
- approves architecture direction when needed
- approves releases and major tradeoffs

The founder does not manually perform every specialist role.
